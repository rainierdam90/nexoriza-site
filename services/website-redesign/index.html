FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a full redesign take?

Most projects are completed in 4–8 weeks, depending on scope. We share the first design concepts within 48 hours of your brief being signed off.

Do I need to provide content and copy?

No. We use AI-assisted copywriting to draft all page copy based on your brand, audience, and goals. You review and approve. Photography and video we source or generate — you don't need to provide anything.

Which technologies do you build on?

We use Next.js, React, and Tailwind CSS for the frontend. For CMS, we typically recommend Sanity or Contentful. Hosting is on Vercel or your preferred provider.

What if I want changes after launch?

We include 3 months of post-launch support in every project. After that, we offer flexible monthly retainers for ongoing optimisation and updates.

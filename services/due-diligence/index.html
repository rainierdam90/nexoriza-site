FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

How does the system handle false positives in sanctions screening?

Our matching algorithm uses entity disambiguation and confidence scoring to dramatically reduce false positives. Each potential match includes a detailed breakdown of match rationale, allowing your compliance officer to make an informed decision in seconds — not hours.

Can the CDD reports be customised to our internal template?

Yes. We onboard each client with a template configuration session. Your report structure, risk categories, language, and escalation thresholds are configured to match your existing processes — so adoption is seamless.

Which languages does the adverse media screening cover?

We cover adverse media in 100+ languages, with AI-powered translation and relevance scoring. Coverage includes major news databases, regional publications, and regulatory announcements.

How does pricing work?

We offer volume-based pricing per review, plus a monthly platform fee that includes monitoring for your existing portfolio. Contact us for a tailored quote based on your review volumes and entity types.

Is the platform suitable for smaller compliance teams?

Absolutely. The platform is designed to give a team of two the output capacity of a team of ten. There's no minimum volume requirement — we work with boutique asset managers and large banks alike.
